How does it work?

Installing an electric car charging station at home is easy with Nash. Once you’ve chosen the lease vehicle that suits you best, our team can provide a referral to Ohme to arrange your charger installation in just a few simple steps.

What are the next steps?

1. Complete the home eligibility survey to assess if you need a standard or custom installation

2. Choose which home charger you require

3. Complete a home survey online to learn about home, energy usage and installation

4. Ohme will get in touch to arrange an installation date

5. Schedule a home visit and discuss the available options

Other considerations

Charger Placement

It is important to be aware of where your charging port will be located on the car, as this can differ between manufacturers and models. This can affect the best placement for your home charging point. If you’re unsure, chat with our team to discuss your best options.
