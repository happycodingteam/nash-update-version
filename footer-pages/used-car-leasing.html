What is Used Car Leasing?

Nash Re-Lease used car leasing allows businesses and private individuals to take advantage of end-of-contract lease cars which have completed their initial lease.�  These used lease cars usually offer great value, as the vehicles have been serviced and maintained to the manufacturers guidelines and returned at the end of the initial lease in line with the British Vehicle Rental and Leasing Association (BVRLA) Fair Wear and Tear Guidelines.

Used lease cars are typically 2, 3, or 4 years old and have been leased on an initial contract and then returned. They therefore offer an affordable option to drive a modern, well-maintained vehicle as a more attractive price than a brand new car.

When you lease a used car in the UK, you can configure your contract exactly the same as you would with a new lease car. So you can have a business contract hire agreement for a company or sole trader and a personal leasing contract for a private individual. You can include your annual mileage and for how long you want to lease the vehicle, typically 1, 2, or 3 years (depending on the age of the vehicle).

This also means that used car leasing works well for those looking for a 12-month contract, if they are looking for a shorter lease for a variety of reasons.

Nash Re-Lease used cars in the UK are fully safety checked and inspected, cleaned and valeted and come with road tax included.

Our team can also provide you with an optional service and maintenance package, which covers all routine servicing, maintenance and tyres (subject to terms and conditions. Your Account Manager will provide you with details on request). � Roadside assistance and breakdown cover is also part of this package. This means that you have complete peace of mind and no unexpected costs during the term of your lease, as well as having a hassle-free and convenient solution to booking your car in for servicing at a time and place convenient to you, using our online portal or one simple phone call.

All vehicles are in stock and can be delivered quickly, once financial approval is in place and the relevant documentation signed and returned.�  Our team are on hand to help make the process as simple as possible.

Your new lease vehicle will be delivered free to you within mainland Great Britain, and collected at the end of the lease agreement.

For business used car leasing agreements, 50% of the VAT can also be reclaimed on a lease agreement.�  This assumes some personal use of the used lease car too.

It’s important to remember that a leasing contract is a non-ownership agreement, so the car is simply collected and handed back at the end of the contract term.� 

Used Car Leasing Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to some of our most frequently asked questions on Used Car Leasing

  • What is the difference between used car leasing and new car leasing?

    Used car leasing works in exactly the same way as leasing a brand new or pre-registered vehicle. The main difference is that the vehicle has completed an initial lease term, and is usually a couple of years old.�  This means it offers an affordable alternative and the vehicle is in stock ready for quick delivery.

  • Is road tax included in used car lease deals?

    All Nash Re-Lease used car lease deals have road tax included for the term of the leasing agreement. Any government increases to the cost of road tax may be passed on to you if this happens during the term of the leasing agreement.

  • What mileages are on used lease cars?

    The mileages on Nash Re-Lease used lease cars vary.�  We will let you know the current mileage of the vehicle in the initial stages, when we provide you a no-obligation quotation. The mileage already on the vehicle is not counted as part of your leasing contract.

  • Where are used car leases sourced from?

    Nash Re-Lease vehicles are carefully selected from our finance providers, which have been completed their initial lease agreement.�  The cars have been service and maintained to the manufacturer’s guidelines, safety-checked and valeted. � They are therefore available for re-lease and can be delivered quickly.

  • How quickly can a used lease car be delivered to me?

    Used car lease deals can be delivered within 14 days.�  In order to be able to deliver the vehicle to you, we will require a finance approval, signed order form, paid vehicle sourcing charge and any required identify check, for example, a copy of your driving licence and a copy of your vehicle insurance.

  • How will my used lease car be delivered?

    Nash Re-Lease vehicles are delivered free within mainland Great Britain.�  They are usually driven directly to your home or business address (depending on your type of contract).�  Deliveries take place weekdays Monday to Friday between the hours of 8am and 6pm.

  • What happens if I exceed the agreed mileage on my used car leasing contract?

    If you exceed the contractual mileage on your used car leasing agreement, an excess mileage charge will be applied at the end of the contract.�  This “pence per mile” cost will be provided to you on your quotation and on your order form, and, if you are in this situation, we can help to work out the cost for you if you give us a call.� 

  • What happens when my used car lease agreement comes to an end?

    At the end of your used car lease agreement, we will arrange collection of the vehicle with you.�  You should ensure that the vehicle is legal and roadworthy and that any servicing and MOT requirements are up-to-date and no warning lights are on the vehicle.�  Tyres should be within the legal limit.�  There should be no missing items (e.g. both cars and parcel shelf should be returned if they were originally provided), and the vehicle will be checked in line with the BVRLA Fair Wear and Tear Guide.�  The vehicle will be photographed before delivery and the start of your contract and again at the end on collection for comparison.

  • Can I return my used lease car early?

    Used car leasing agreements are for a specified fixed term, so if the vehicle were to be returned before the end of the agreement it would be subject to an early termination charge outlined in your contract.�  Typically, this could be 50% of any outstanding rentals for the remainder of the term, but can differ by vehicle and finance provider so please ask us for an individual early termination quote.

  • Can I look at the used lease cars before I place my order?

    Our friendly and professional team will provide you with photos and information on your preferred used lease vehicle.�  Unfortunately, the cars cannot be viewed in person before you place your order as many of them are held in a secure area once they have been returned from their initial lease which is not open to the public.�  However, you will be able to inspect the vehicle on delivery, prior to accepting it
